Who We Are

In 1875, we founded the first Bun Buns Vegan Bakes in Pittsburgh. Since then, we have remained the only vegan only bakery in this city.

All of our ingredients are ethically sourced and organic. Our bakers make every piece of dessert in the morning at 5am, so we promise to deliver only the highest quality items. Every pastry we make is a labor of love.
